About

Oi! My name is Fernando Diaz (he/él), and I am currently a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Mathematics Department at Fitchburg State University, MA. I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Tulane University in August 2024, under the guidance of Mahir B. Can.

My research interests encompass a broad range of topics in algebraic groups and combinatorics.

Prior to my Ph.D., I obtained my M.S. from ESFM-IPN in Ciudad de México and my B.S. from Universidad Sergio Arboleda in Bogotá. Outside of mathematics, I enjoy playing soccer, running, cycling, and exploring nature.
